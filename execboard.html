EXECUTIVE BOARD

UNSC

Taher Penwala

Co-chair

An outdoorsy individual and MUN enthusiast who you'd spot memorising speeches in the middle of traffic congestions. However gawky he may sound, it's just a deceiving cover for his sly ways of making things work his way. That's Taher Penwala in a nutshell.

He's a broad minded individual who is always up for deep discussions. An active participant in whatever task it may be, he'd always have his own quirky ways but will make sure get the best out of it.

Feel free to ask multiple doubts at any point, as many times as you wish as he's extremely approachable too.

Krisha Kalsi

Director General

Curiosity. Teamwork. Quick-thinking.

These are the words Krisha would choose to describe what being a MUNer encompasses. Having attended many conferences herself, Krisha believes that MUNs are one of those rare and life-changing opportunities that will open your eyes to the world and make you well-versed with grappling issues in the most fun and interactive way possible!

Krisha is glad to serve as DYMUN’s Director-General this year. She strives to guide and empower delegates every step of the way, whether it is their first conference or yet another feather in their MUN cap. Along with her amazing and ardent team, she is preparing a conference to remember!

Feel free to contact her if you have any questions or queries. Krisha can’t wait to see you at the DYMUN conference!

Tisya Goel

Charge D'affaires

With an effervescent personality, Tisya Goel is very excited to be DYMUN’s Charge D’affaires. She did her first MUN conference in grade 7, and has been chasing that adrenaline rush of a good debate ever since.

Critical thinking and devising instant jabs are Tisya’s favourite aspects of a MUN. She believes that MUNs shape young students' minds in a way like no other extracurricular activity and compose the youth to be much more aware of the happenings around the world. She is eager to help other delegates in every way she can, and aspires to make students comfortable and master MUN skills.

She is looking forward to new and exciting debate-filled adventures with the delegates!

Manasi Sonavane

Chief Advisor

Accept both compliments and criticism, knowing it takes both rain and sun to make a flower bloom. Manasi Sonavane, a passionate debater, has pulled herself up to do various MUNs and organise them with tonnes of interest.

For the upcoming event, she will put her soul into making you comfortable and appreciated in the debate session. As Caroline Forbes said- “When anybody tells me I can’t do something, I prove them wrong” is exactly what defines Manasi’s passion with everything she does.

She will make sure that each and everyone in her committee gets inspired to participate in the debate. not only during conferences but also in life. She will not only make debating seem like a great opportunity, but also as a challenge for those adventurers who want to look at perspectives differently. Manasi aims to shape you into your own role model, and will always be there to hold your hand and walk with you through all your milestones.

Shreesh Shirekar

Tech Head

Shreesh Shirekar is an inquisitive IBDP Y1 student, and is thrilled to be DYMUN’s Tech Head for 2021. Having attended many conferences and debates himself, Shreesh knows that MUNs are an exhilarating and action-filled experience that everyone should take part in!

Shreesh will lead the IT team remarkably, and certifies that the entire DYMUN experience will be technically sound - right from the website to the conference itself!
He will make sure DYMUN 2021 is an MUN to remember!
