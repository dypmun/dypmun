Harshiv Gajjar

Co-chair

Accept both compliments and criticism, knowing it takes both rain and sun to make a flower bloom. Manasi Sonavane, a passionate debater, has pulled herself up to do various MUNs and organise them with tonnes of interest.

Tisya Goel

Co-chair

With an effervescent personality, Tisya Goel is very excited to be DYMUN’s Charge D’affaires. She did her first MUN conference in grade 7, and has been chasing that adrenaline rush of a good debate ever since.

Critical thinking and devising instant jabs are Tisya’s favourite aspects of a MUN. She believes that MUNs shape young students' minds in a way like no other extracurricular activity and compose the youth to be much more aware of the happenings around the world. She is eager to help other delegates in every way she can, and aspires to make students comfortable and master MUN skills.

She is looking forward to new and exciting debate-filled adventures with the delegates!
